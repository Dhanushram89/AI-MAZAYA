International Student Identity Card

Maximize your student experience with ISIC

Introducing the International Student Identity Card (ISIC), your ultimate companion for an enriching student experience worldwide.The ISIC is globally recognized as the only internationally accepted proof of student status. It verifies your identity as a student and opens doors to a wide range of exclusive opportunities and services.

The ISIC card serves as a recognized form of identification, not only for discounts but also for accessing various student services. From university libraries to student health centers, the ISIC ensures you receive the benefits and services you're entitled to as a student.
